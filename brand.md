# Brand - LiveAssist AI

Status: active

LiveAssist AI is a private real-time knowledge assistant for customer-facing teams. The brand should feel premium, fast, trustworthy, and business-friendly.

## Visual Direction

- Warm, editorial, premium desktop-product interface: warm off-white base (`#faf8f5`), soft frosted/warm cards, generous negative space. Calm and quiet — not techy or saturated.
- Backgrounds are warm neutrals — NEVER a blue/lavender wash. Warm surfaces: base `#faf8f5`, cards `#ffffff`, soft tints `#f6f2ec` / `#efe9e0`, warm hairline `#e9e3da`.
- Warm GOLD/amber is the accent (inspired by the golden-dog app logo — "crystalline / tender"). Two tones for contrast:
  - Button fill = soft light "crystalline" gold gradient `#fff0c4 → #ffd27a → #f5b23f` with DARK warm text `#4a2f08` (light gold needs dark text). Glassy top highlight, warm inner depth.
  - Text/icon accents (eyebrows, links, nav-active, step numbers, stat numbers) = readable deep amber `#a35707`; small dots/rings/decorative fills = gold `#c9820f` / `rgba(217,152,30,…)`.
- Text is warm: near-black `#1a1917`, muted `#6b665e`, emphasized `#423d36`.
- Buttons are calm and refined (soft shadow, glassy sheen) — not glossy or shouting. Rounded rectangles, not pills.
- Hero/section content sits in soft frosted or warm cards with soft shadows and minimal/no border.
- Semantic colors stay: green = success/live (product mockup), traffic-light dots in the demo.
- Avoid violet/purple, teal/mint, cool electric blue as accent, blue/lavender background washes, generic SaaS gradients, spyware/surveillance framing.

## Voice

- Clear, sharp, and concrete.
- Focus on hotkey workflow, company documents, fast answers, source citations, and confidence during live conversations.
- Avoid vague AI hype.
